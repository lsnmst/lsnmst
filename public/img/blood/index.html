LSNMST +

My real name is Alessandro (Italian) My surname corresponds to the name of the woman who sings "Quando men vo" in Giacomo Puccini's La Boheme to the painter Marcello at the tables of Caffè Momus.
This portfolio is fancy because the "creative industry" only seems to be into this genre. I went to GitHub, found Luis Henrique Bizarro's portfolio, and there we are.

Superpower

Since we are human beings surrounded by an immensurable complexity, we couldn't terraform Mars in a matter a few human generations, or surgically engineer the climate without a torrent of unintended consequences. If you need short and simple solutions first you must be able to see the complexity. My superpower is to turn simple into complex, even though to many complex sounds or is mistaken for complicated.

Commitment

If you've spent at least a minute of your valuable time browsing through the portfolio entries, you've already figured out that I care about the health of the planet and the ecological justice for the human communities that inhabit it. If you've thought about it at least once, we got something to talk about.

METROCARTOGRAPHY

Ally / Partner Polytechnic University of Milan (Italy)

Role Researcher · Front-end web developer

Year (s) 2019

Live URL Online course. City is a complex, adaptive, and closed-boundary system that continuously transform itself. It has been conceived as dynamic systems subject to optimization. A series of new integrative approaches have allowed advancement ind re-conceptualising city as a complex system and the developing a scientific practice framed by the theory of complexity. These practices have been implemented in city management, to control the city as a whole, to supervise phenomena and to lead evolution. The metropolitan cartography is the process of reading, understanding, and interpreting the territory of a metropolis using maps and spatial information technologies in a decision-making support system.
